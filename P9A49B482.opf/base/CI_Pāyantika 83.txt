yaḥ punar bhikṣur anvardhamāsaṃ prātimokṣasūtre uddiśyamāne evaṃ vaded idānīm ahaṃ jānāmi āyuṣmanto
’yam api kila dharmaḥ sūtragataḥ sūtraparyāyāpannoddeśyam āgacchatīti tatrāyuṣmantaṃ
ced bhikṣavo jānīyuḥ niṣaṇṇapūrvaṃ āyuṣmatā dvitrikṛtvaṃ poṣadhe kaḥ punar vādo bhūya iti
āyuṣmato ajñānataḥ na muktiḥ yata āpattim āpannaḥ tato yathādharmaṃ kārayitavya uttaraṃ
ca kaukṛtyam āropayitavyaṃ -- āyuṣman te alābho ’labdhaḥ durlabdho na sulabdho yat tvaṃ anvardhamāsaṃ
prātimokṣasūtroddiśyamāne na satkṛtya śṛṇoṣi na gurukaroṣi nārthikaroṣi na manasikaroṣi
ekāgracittenāvahitaśrotreṇa na śṛṇoṣi sarvacittasaṃkalpair api na śṛṇoṣi | āyuṣman
tatra kaukṛtyāt pāyantikā |
