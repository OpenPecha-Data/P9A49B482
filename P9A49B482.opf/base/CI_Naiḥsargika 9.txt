bhikṣuṃ khalūddiśyājñātinā gṛhapatinā gṛhapatipatnyā ca pratyekapratyekāni cīvaracetanakāni
pratyupasthāpitāni syuḥ | ebhir āvāṃ pratyekapratyekaiḥ cīvaracetanakair evaṃrūpaṃ caivaṃrūpaṃ
ca pratyekapratyekaṃ cīvaraṃ cetayitvā evaṃnāmā bhikṣur upasaṃkramiṣyati tam ācchādayiṣyāvaḥ
| pratyekapratyekābhyāṃ cīvaracetanakābhyāṃ kāle kalpikābhyām iti | tatra cet sa bhikṣuḥ
pūrvam apravāritaḥ san kaṃcid eva vikalpam āpatya tam ajñātigṛhapatiṃ gṛhapatipatnīṃ
vopasaṃkramyaivaṃ vaded yāni tāny āyuṣmatyātmān uddiśya pratyekapratyekāni cīvaracetanakāni
pratyupasthāpitāni | sādhyāyuṣmatau tau pratyekapratyekaiś cīvaracetanakair evaṃrūpaṃ
caivaṃrūpaṃ ca cīvaraṃ cetayitvā ācchādayatām ubhāv api bhūtvā ekaikena cīvareṇa kāle kalpikena
kalyāṇakāmatām upādāyābhiniṣpanne cīvare naisargikā pāyantikā |
