yaḥ punar bhikṣur dviṣṭoddeśād apratītaḥ śuddhaṃ bhikṣuṃ anyasya vā anyathābhāgīyasya adhikaraṇasya
kañcid eva leśamātraṃ dharmam upādāya pārājikena dharmeṇa anudhvaṃsayed apy evainaṃ
brahmacaryāc cyāvayeyam iti tasya ca apareṇa samayena samanuyujyamānasya vā samanuyujyamānasya
vā anyabhāgīyaṃ tad adhikaraṇaṃ bhavati kaścid eva leśoddeśamātro dharma upātto bhavati
bhikṣuś ca dveṣe pratiṣṭhed dveṣād avocam iti saṃghāvaśeṣaḥ |
