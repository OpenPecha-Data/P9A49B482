yaḥ punar bhikṣur antagṛhaṃ piṇḍāya carantyā ajñātyā bhikṣuṇyāḥ santikāt sahastaṃ khādanīyabhojanīyaṃ
pratigṛhya khāded vā bhuñjīta vā tena bhikṣuṇā bahirārāmaṃ gatvā bhikṣor antike
pratideśayitavyam garhyam asmy āyuṣmantaḥ sthānam āpanno ’sātmyaṃ pratideśanīyaṃ taṃ dharmaṃ
pratideśayāmīti ayam api dharmaḥ pratideśanīyaḥ |
