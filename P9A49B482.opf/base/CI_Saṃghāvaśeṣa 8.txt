yaḥ punar bhikṣur dviṣṭoddeśād apratītaḥ śuddhaṃ bhikṣuṃ amūlakena pārājikena dharmeṇānudhvaṃsayed
apy evainaṃ brahmacaryāc cyāvayeyam iti tasya ca apareṇa samayena samanuyujyamāno vā
asamanuyujyamāno vā amūlakam eva svādhikaraṇaṃ bhaved bhikṣuś ca dveṣe pratiṣṭhed dveṣeṇāvocam
iti saṃghāvaśeṣaḥ |
