yāni tāni kāni śayanāsanāni sāśaṃkasaṃmatāni sabhayasaṃmatāni sapratibhayabhairavasaṃmatāni
yaḥ punar bhikṣus tadrūpeṣu saṃghasyāraṇyakeṣu śayaneṣu sāśaṃkasaṃmateṣu sabhayasaṃmateṣu
sapratibhayabhairavasaṃmateṣu pūrvam apratisaṃvidyate vane bahirārāmasya khādanīyabhojanīyaṃ
khāded bhuṃjīta vā tena bhikṣuṇā bhikṣūṇām antike pratideśayitavyaṃ garhyam asmy āyuṣmantaḥ
sthānam āpanno ’sātmyaṃ pratideśanīyaṃ taṃ dharmaṃ pratideśayāmīti ayam api dharmaḥ pratideśanīyaḥ
